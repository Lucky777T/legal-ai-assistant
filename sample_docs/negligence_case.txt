IN THE SUPREME COURT OF [STATE]

Case No: SC-2023-001
Plaintiff: John Doe
Defendant: ABC Corporation

JUDGMENT ON NEGLIGENCE

The Court finds that to establish negligence, a plaintiff must prove:
1. The defendant owed a duty of care to the plaintiff
2. The defendant breached that duty
3. The breach caused harm to the plaintiff
4. The plaintiff suffered damages as a result

In this case, the defendant corporation failed to maintain safe premises, 
which constituted a breach of duty. The plaintiff suffered injuries as a direct result.

The elements of negligence are well-established in common law and have been 
consistently applied in jurisdictions following the English legal tradition.

AWARD: The Court awards damages in the amount of $500,000 to the plaintiff.
